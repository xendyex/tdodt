How to Play

Objective

In "Try Dying or Die Trying!", your objective is simple: survive as long as possible, kill the enemies, and have fun! (New stuff soon)

Controls

Use the arrow keys or WASD to move your character. Press the up arrow to jump over obstacles and gaps. Avoid touching any hazards or falling into pits!

Scoring

Your level increases as you progress... There'll be more soon!
