How to Play

Objective

In "Try Dying or Die Trying!", your objective is simple: survive as long as possible, kill the enemies, and have fun! (New stuff soon)

Controls

Use the arrow keys or WASD to move your character. Avoid the enemies, and click to fire your gun! Press R to reload.

Press 1 and 2 to switch guns. For now.

Scoring

Your level increases as you progress... There'll be more soon!
